Chicken Soup with spinach

Ingredients:

For the soup:

  • 250 ml chicken filet
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1.5l bouillon
  • 200g spinach
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 250g yogurt
  • 250g yogurt
  • 50g sunflower oil
  • 10g sault
  • 5g white pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in the saucepan and fry the chopped onion and garlic together with the chicken. Cut into cubes. Add the grated carrot. And finally add the spinach.
  2. Pour the broth in and let it bouil for 30 mins.
  3. Mix the yolks with the yogurt in bowl, skim off the warm soup and add a few ladles hot broth to the mix for balancing the temperature of the soup. And after that return everything into the saucepan.

ENJOY!
